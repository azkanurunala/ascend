Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

No account · No personal profile

This Privacy Policy explains how Ascend ("the app", "we", "us") handles information. The short version: Ascend has no accounts and no sign-in, and your gameplay progress stays on your device. Ascend shows no ads. To sell the optional "Ascend Pro" upgrade and to offer an optional global leaderboard, the app uses a couple of well-known service providers, described below.

Information we collect

Ascend itself does not ask you for your name, email, or any account details. We do not build a profile of you, and there is no advertising or tracking.

The optional features below rely on third-party services that may collect limited technical data:

  • In-app purchase (Apple & RevenueCat). If you buy Ascend Pro, the transaction is processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to validate and restore the purchase; it receives a purchase receipt and an anonymous app-generated identifier. We never see your card details.
  • Leaderboard (Apple Game Center). If you're signed in to Game Center, your high score, Game Center player ID, and public display name are used by Apple to rank you on the leaderboards. You can turn Game Center off in iOS Settings.

If you never make a purchase and don't use Game Center, the app collects no data and works fully offline.

Data stored on your device

To remember your progress, Ascend saves the following locally on your device only — it never leaves your phone and we cannot see it:

  • Your best height and game statistics (runs played, time in the air)
  • Which orb skins you own and which one is equipped
  • Your settings (sound, haptics, reduced motion, quality)

Deleting the app removes this data permanently.

Children's privacy

Ascend is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. There is no advertising or tracking, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-party services

We use the following providers only for the optional features described above. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • RevenueCat — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store & Game Center — apple.com/legal/privacy

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
