How to play

  • Tap anywhere to flap the orb upward against gravity.
  • Release and it falls — find the rhythm.
  • Thread the gaps in the frosted-glass pillars. One miss ends the run.
  • Climb through eight altitude bands — Meadow, Open Sky, High Sky, Stratosphere, Mesosphere, Aurora, The Edge, and Orbit.
  • Unlock luminous orb skins and chase your personal best height.

Common questions

Does Ascend need the internet?
No. The game is fully offline. No account, no sign-in, no connection required.

Where is my progress saved?
Everything — your best height, runs, playtime, owned skins, and settings — is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app clears it.

Does it collect any of my data?
No ads, no tracking. Data is only involved if you buy Ascend Pro (via Apple/RevenueCat) or use the Game Center leaderboard. Play offline and none of it is used. See our Privacy Policy.

I found a bug or have an idea.
Please email us — it genuinely helps.
